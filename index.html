What is BASIRA?

A little 2D platformer game with pixel art graphics. Based on the Godot Engine, I develop this game for the purpose of an exam for a developer title in my school.

But why did you start making a game ?

In fact, I've always been drawn to making games since I was a kid. My nights of video games (as well as my days) made me want to move to the other side of the screen, and start creating something that is mine.

Why this website ? And where I can play the GAME ?

This site is made to share with you my progress in a more serious way than all my other (abandoned) projects. Expect the site to be updated with each of the various updates that will be made on the game. As you will understand, the game is in development at the moment and no playable version is available.
